What is the goal?

A large amount of housing and property data is available to the public, however, drawing useful conclusions from this data can be a challenge. Our goal is to make it easier for prospective homeowners, current homeowners, and other members of the community to learn about housing trends in their area.

How does it work?

Users may understandably be interested in knowing the trends in a given neighborhood or zip code. Learning about the different attributes of the communities we create better informed citizens and better communities. Our goals regarding trend analysis are to make it easy for users to identify patterns in the data, deviations from those patterns, and objectively make comparisons between two groups using predetermined queries.
