Query 3

The ‘Save Our Homes Act’ is an amendment to the Florida constitution which limits the increase in the taxable value of homesteaded properties to three percent or the change in the National Consumer Price Index, whichever is less. Critics have argued that this is unfair since it allows homeowners to purchase a property, wait for the value to rise, and sell at a high market value while having paid a much lower tax rate. In this query, we are interested in determining whether there is any truth to the claim that the ‘Save Our Homes Act’ is being exploited to purchase more expensive homes.
